Welcome to Our Organization

Think Rich to Alleviate Mediocrity and Poverty out of Liberia(TRAMP-LIBERIA) is a Non-Profit Organization with a commitment to uplift individuals and communities by harnessing their skills and abilities.

We aim to inspire excellence and creativity, promoting a culture where talents are recognized and utilized to combat social issues like poverty and Mediocrity.
Our Vision reflects a desire to empower people to rise above Mediocrity, encouraging them to leverage their talents for personal growth and community improvement.

Our Mission

To dismantle poverty and mediocrity by awakening and equipping young people with their
God-given talents, cultivating lives of purpose, faith, and excellence. We exist to empower
youth from underserved communities through mentorship, creativity, and leadership, guiding
them to rise as transformational voices in society—called not just to succeed, but to serve.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to break the cycles of poverty and mediocrity by awakening and
equipping young people with purpose, faith, and talent. We are a faith-based
nonprofit dedicated to nurturing the God-given gifts of youth, empowering them
to rise above limitations and become agents of transformation in their
communities and beyond.

Vision

We envision a world where no young person is trapped by poverty or limited by low
expectations. Through holistic mentorship, creative empowerment, and Christ-centered
leadership, we aim to raise a generation of purpose-driven youth who overcome,
uplift, and inspire—changing the world not by chance, but by calling

Our Core Values

Thoughts: We believe that our thoughts builds our reality and creativity
Education: We believe that Education is that bridge that takes talent from base to across
Faith First: We trust in God’s purpose and center all we do on biblical principles.
Excellence Over Mediocrity: We challenge the norm and cultivate greatness in every endeavor.
Empowerment Through Talent: We believe gifts are tools for both personal breakthrough and community impact.
Compassionate Leadership: We raise leaders who serve with empathy, integrity, and vision.
Poverty Disruption: We are relentless in confronting economic, mental, and spiritual poverty with hope and opportunity.
Community Impact: We’re rooted in grassroots change and believe transformation begins at home.
